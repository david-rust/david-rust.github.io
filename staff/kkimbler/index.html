Biography

About Kimberly

Kimberly Kimbler serves as Casefinding Auditor for the Kentucky Cancer Registry, conducting systematic audits of reporting facilities to verify that all reportable cancer cases are being identified and submitted to KCR in accordance with state law and national standards.

Her auditing work is critical to maintaining the completeness and reliability of Kentucky's statewide cancer data.
