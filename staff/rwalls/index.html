Biography

About Robin

Robin Walls serves as Senior Regional Coordinator for the Kentucky Cancer Registry, partnering with non-hospital reporting facilities in her region to promote accurate and timely cancer case submissions.

Her ongoing relationships with regional facilities are key to KCR's statewide data completeness.
