Biography

About David

David Rust is a Programmer and Systems Analyst with the Kentucky Cancer Registry's Cancer Informatics division. He supports the development and maintenance of software applications central to KCR's cancer data operations.

His day-to-day responsibilities include building and refining software components, analyzing system requirements, troubleshooting technical issues, and contributing to the continuous improvement of KCR's informatics infrastructure. David works collaboratively with senior developers and registry staff to deliver reliable, well-tested software solutions.

His work helps ensure that KCR's data systems remain current, accurate, and capable of meeting the demands of a statewide cancer surveillance program.

Peer-Reviewed Work

2023
DeepPhe-CR: Natural Language Processing Software Services for Cancer Registrar Case Abstraction
Hochheiser H, Finan S, Yuan Z, Durbin EB, Jeong JC, Hands I, Rust D, et al. — JCO Clinical Cancer Informatics
Presents NLP software services designed to assist cancer registrars in automating case abstraction from clinical pathology reports, reducing manual abstraction burden.
2019
Deep Transfer Learning Across Cancer Registries for Information Extraction from Pathology Reports
Alawad M, Gao S, Qiu J, Schaefferkoetter N, Hinkle JD, Yoon HJ, Wu XC, Durbin EB, Jeong JC, Hands I, Rust D, et al. — IEEE EMBS Int Conf Biomed Health Inform
