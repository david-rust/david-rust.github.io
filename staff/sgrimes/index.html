Biography

About Scott

Scott Grimes holds the position of Systems Professional III at the Kentucky Cancer Registry, where he focuses on the administration, configuration, and security of the IT infrastructure supporting KCR's operations.

His responsibilities include server management, network configuration, security compliance, and the reliability of KCR's production systems. Scott works to ensure that KCR's computing environment meets the rigorous security and privacy standards required for a HIPAA-covered entity handling sensitive cancer patient data.

He plays a key role in disaster recovery planning, system monitoring, and the procurement and lifecycle management of KCR's hardware and software assets.
