Biography

About Tracy

Tracy Sumler serves as QA Manager II for Education, Training, and Auditing at the Kentucky Cancer Registry, leading programs that train cancer registrars and auditing reporting facilities to ensure consistent data quality across the Commonwealth.

Her educational and audit programs strengthen the skills of KCR's reporting network and support continuous quality improvement.
