Biography

About Ivelina

Ivelina Todorova provides administrative support to the Kentucky Cancer Registry's Executive Leadership team, contributing to the operational efficiency of Kentucky's statewide cancer surveillance program.

Her attention to detail and organizational skills help ensure that KCR's administrative functions run smoothly in support of the registry's public health mission.
