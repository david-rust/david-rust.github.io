Biography

About Paula

Paula Cole provides administrative support to the Kentucky Cancer Registry's Executive Leadership team, coordinating scheduling, communications, and office operations to ensure the registry's mission is carried out effectively.

Her organizational expertise contributes to the smooth day-to-day operations of KCR's statewide cancer surveillance program.
