Biography

About Evan

Evan Yangg is a Programmer and Systems Analyst with the Kentucky Cancer Registry's Cancer Informatics division. He supports the development and maintenance of software applications central to KCR's cancer data operations.

His day-to-day responsibilities include building and refining software components, analyzing system requirements, troubleshooting technical issues, and contributing to the continuous improvement of KCR's informatics infrastructure. Evan works collaboratively with senior developers and registry staff to deliver reliable, well-tested software solutions.

His work helps ensure that KCR's data systems remain current, accurate, and capable of meeting the demands of a statewide cancer surveillance program.
